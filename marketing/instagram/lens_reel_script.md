# Sankofa Lens — Reel Template & Script (+ campaign versions)

Your most shareable pillar: show the app naming real objects in Twi. Branded frames drop into any editor (CapCut, Reels) + a shot-by-shot script.

**Files in `reel/`:** `1_cover.png` (hook), `2_label_food.png` / `3_label_water.png` / `4_label_epono.png` (transparent overlay "word cards" to lay over your footage), `5_endcard.png` (CTA close).
Add an object: add `label_card("Twi","English","6_label_x")` in `generate_highlights_reels.py` and re-run.

**Campaign versions in `campaign/`:** `worldcup_cover.png` + `worldcup_endcard.png` (Ghana vs Colombia — "Can you talk noise in Twi?" / "Yɛbɛdi nkonim!"), `detty_cover.png` + `detty_endcard.png` (Detty December — "Traveling to Ghana? Learn Twi." / "Land speaking.").

## Format
9:16, 1080×1920, 12–20s. Hook in the first 1s. Burn captions in. Trending afrobeats/amapiano low under your voice.
