## 2. Product overview

Sankofa Twi helps you learn Akan/Twi through bite-sized lessons, Adinkra symbol
study, AI translation, a camera "point-and-learn" tool, and gamified streaks and
leaderboards. "Sankofa" means *go back and fetch it* — reclaiming language and
heritage.

**Where things live:** Tap the **Menu** dropdown (top-left) to switch between
Progress, Journey, Symbols (home), Lessons, Translate, Lens, Day Name, Quiz, and
Leaderboard. Tap your **avatar** (top-right) for Profile and settings.

## 3. Getting started & your account

### Create an account
- Open the app and choose **Create account**.
- Enter a valid **email** and a **password** (at least 6 characters).
- Solve the quick **security check** (a simple sum).
- Tick **"I agree to the Terms & Privacy Policy"** (required).
- Tap **Create account**. We send a **verification email** — open it to confirm.
- You can also use **Sign in with Google**.

**Each email can be used once.** If you see *"An account already exists for that
email,"* tap **Sign in with this email** to log in instead.

### Verify your email
- After signing up, check your inbox (and spam) for the confirmation link.
- Didn't get it? Go to **Profile** → tap **Resend** on the verify-email banner.

### Forgot password
- On the sign-in screen, tap **Forgot password?**, enter your email, and we'll
  send a reset link.

### Choosing a plan
- New accounts see a one-time **Free vs Premium** choice. You can change this
  anytime in **Profile → Subscription**.

## 4. Lessons, XP & streaks

- **Lessons / Journey:** Work through lessons in order; each completion earns
  **XP** and unlocks the next step.
- **Levels & leagues:** XP raises your level and your league — **Asɛmpa** (good
  news) → **Nkɔsoɔ** (progress) → **Ahenfie** (the royal court).
- **Daily quests:** Finish a lesson, earn 50 XP, and ace a quiz each day.
- **Streaks:** Practise daily to grow your streak and weave your **kente cloth**.
- **Wisdom keys & treasure chest:** Chain correct answers to earn keys; collect
  3 to open a chest (rewards streak freezes).
- **Streak freeze:** Protects your streak on a day you miss. Earn them from
  chests or buy one for **50 pedis** (Progress → Pedis wallet).

## 6. AI Translate

- Open **Translate** from the Menu.
- Choose direction: **English → Twi** or **Twi → English**.
- Type your text and tap **Translate**.
- Tap **Listen (Twi)** to hear the Twi audio.

**Notes:**
- AI Translate is a **Premium** feature.
- Each translation and each audio play uses **1 AI credit** (see Section 8).
- Replaying audio you've already heard this session is **free** (it's cached).

## 7. Sankofa Lens (point, learn, speak)

Lens names real-world objects in Twi so you can learn — and communicate — on the
go (great when travelling in Ghana).

**How to use it:**
- Open **Lens** from the Menu and tap **Point & capture** (or **Gallery**).
- Lens recognises the object and shows its **Twi name**.
- Tap **Say it out loud** to play the Twi audio — show and speak it to a Twi
  speaker.
- Wrong guess? Tap one of the **alternative labels** to re-translate (free).
- Tap **Save** to add it to your **visual dictionary**.

**Collections & badges:**
- Saved objects fill themed **Collections** (Animals, Food & drink, Home,
  Nature, Travel & street, People & style).
- Milestones earn **badges** (First find → Explorer → Collector → Curator). Your
  first save of an object earns **+10 pedis**.

**Notes:**
- A scan and each audio play use **1 AI credit** (see Section 8). Re-hearing a
  saved word is **free** after the first play.
- Recognition runs **on your device** — your photos aren't uploaded for this.
- During the test launch, Lens is open to everyone ("Premium · free in testing").

## 8. AI credits & pedis

### AI credits
- AI Translate, Lens scans, and audio all draw from **one monthly pool**.
  **1 credit = 1 AI action** (a translation, a scan, or a fresh audio play).
- **Included each month:** Premium **400**, Free **15**. Credits reset monthly.
- Run out? **Top up with pedis** — **10 credits for 20 pedis** — or wait for the
  monthly reset.
- Replaying audio you've already played is **free** (cached), so it won't use a
  credit.

### Pedis (soft currency)
- **Earn** pedis by completing lessons, keeping streaks, and saving first finds
  in Lens.
- **Spend** pedis on: AI credit top-ups, streak freezes (50), and avatars.
- **Buy** pedis (coming soon, with paid plans): 100 for €0.99, 550 for €4.99,
  1,200 for €9.99. Prices show in your local currency; pedis have no cash value.
- See your balance in **Profile → Pedis** or **Progress → Pedis wallet**.

## 12. Troubleshooting

> **Protocol:** First ask the user for their **device type** (Android model + OS
> version) and a **brief description of the error**. Then offer the 3 most likely
> fixes below.

### Translation or Lens isn't working / "failed"
1. **Check your internet connection** and try again.
2. The translator may be **waking up** (our server sleeps when idle) — wait
   ~30–60 seconds and **retry** once.
3. If it says **"out of AI credits,"** wait for the monthly reset or **top up
   with pedis** (Section 8).

### Twi audio won't play
1. Check your **connection** and **device volume** (not on silent).
2. Make sure you have **AI credits** left (the first play of a word uses one).
3. Tap **Retry** / play again — a momentary network hiccup is common.

### Lens camera won't open
1. Enable the **Camera** permission for Sankofa Twi in **Settings → Apps →
   Permissions**.
2. Try **Gallery** instead to confirm recognition works.
3. Restart the app after changing the permission.

### Can't create an account
1. The **email may already be in use** — tap **Sign in with this email**.
2. Password must be **at least 6 characters**; solve the **security check**; and
   **tick the Terms** checkbox.
3. Check your connection and try again.

### Didn't receive the verification email
1. Check your **spam/junk** folder.
2. **Profile → Resend** the verification email.
3. Confirm the email address is spelled correctly (you can update it in Profile).

### Premium/subscription won't activate
- Paid plans are **launching soon**; the subscribe button currently shows
  "coming soon." No charge is taken yet. We'll announce when billing is live.

### A lesson, streak, or pedis count looks wrong
1. **Pull to refresh** the Progress screen.
2. Fully **close and reopen** the app.
3. If it persists, escalate with the user's device and a screenshot.
